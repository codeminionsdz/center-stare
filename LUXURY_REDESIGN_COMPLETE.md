### Smooth Luxury Animations
- **fade-in-up**: Smooth entrance with cubic-bezier easing

- **scale-in**: Zoom entrances with premium easing

- **shimmer**: Luxury shimmer animations

- **Default**: 300ms (micro-interactions)
